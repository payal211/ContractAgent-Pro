EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement is made effective as of January 1, 2024, between 
Innovative Tech Corp ("Company") and John Smith ("Employee").

POSITION:
Employee is hired as Senior Software Engineer reporting to the CTO.

COMPENSATION:
Employee shall receive an annual salary of $120,000, payable bi-weekly.
Employee is eligible for annual performance bonus up to 20% of base salary.

BENEFITS:
Company will provide health insurance, dental coverage, and 401(k) matching.

TERM:
This is an at-will employment agreement with no specified end date.

TERMINATION:
Either party may terminate employment with or without cause upon 2 weeks notice.

CONFIDENTIALITY:
Employee agrees to maintain confidentiality of all proprietary information 
during and after employment.

NON-COMPETE:
Employee agrees not to work for direct competitors for 12 months after termination 
within a 50-mile radius.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY:
All inventions, patents, and work product created during employment belong to Company.

GOVERNING LAW:
This agreement is governed by California employment law.
