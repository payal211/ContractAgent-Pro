SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on March 15, 2024, between 
TechConsult Solutions LLC, a Delaware limited liability company ("Service Provider") 
and MegaCorp Industries Inc., a California corporation ("Client").

SERVICES:
Service Provider agrees to provide software development and consulting services 
including but not limited to system architecture, code development, and technical support.

COMPENSATION:
Client agrees to pay Service Provider $15,000 per month plus expenses. 
Payment shall be made within 45 days of invoice receipt.

TERM:
This agreement shall commence on April 1, 2024, and continue indefinitely 
until terminated by either party.

TERMINATION:
Either party may terminate this agreement for any reason with 10 days notice.

LIABILITY:
Service Provider shall be liable for all damages, losses, and expenses 
resulting from breach of this agreement, including consequential damages.

CONFIDENTIALITY:
Both parties acknowledge they may have access to confidential information 
and agree to maintain strict confidentiality.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY:
All work product created under this agreement shall belong to Service Provider.

GOVERNING LAW:
This agreement shall be governed by the laws of New York.

DISPUTE RESOLUTION:
Any disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in New York City.
