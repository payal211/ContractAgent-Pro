COMMERCIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Lease Agreement is entered into on February 1, 2024, between 
Downtown Properties LLC ("Landlord") and Coffee Corner Cafe ("Tenant").

PREMISES:
Landlord leases to Tenant the commercial space located at 123 Main Street, 
Suite 100, comprising approximately 1,200 square feet.

TERM:
Lease term is 3 years, commencing March 1, 2024, and ending February 28, 2027.

RENT:
Monthly rent is $3,500, due on the 1st of each month.
Late fee of $50 applies after 5-day grace period.

SECURITY DEPOSIT:
Tenant shall pay security deposit of $7,000 upon signing.

USE:
Premises shall be used solely for cafe and restaurant operations.

MAINTENANCE:
Tenant responsible for interior maintenance; Landlord responsible for structural.

TERMINATION:
Either party may terminate with 60 days written notice for material breach.

GOVERNING LAW:
This lease is governed by the laws of the State of California.
